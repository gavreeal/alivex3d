ALIVE Classmate

ALIVE Classmate is an online virtual classroom environment which brings on-campus and off-campus students together in the one area. The classroom is complete with whiteboard, slide projector, streaming video and webcam and voice-over-ip (VoIP).

 

 

 

 

Download Classmate Beta Version 0.4 Here

Features

  • User represented in 3D world environment as a humanoid character.

  • Real time voice over the internet (VoIP) conferencing.

  • Real time text chat.

  • Avatar Editor for personalising your character.

  • Broadcast Slide Project for giving real time, multi-user slide presentations.

  • Multi-user synchronous whiteboard.

  • Streaming and broadcast video.

  • Live web cam displaying.

  • Auto gesticulation of avatar when talking; this means your avatar moves and gestures with its hands automatically when it detects you are talking into the microphone while using VoIP.
